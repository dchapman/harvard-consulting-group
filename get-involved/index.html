Get Involved

The Harvard Graduate Volunteer Consulting Group (VCG) is looking for enthusiastic candidates as associates for future engagements. The VCG was founded in 2008 and has provided consulting services to over a dozen clients, local and international, from small startups to publicly traded multi-million dollar companies, and on topics from business model development to promoting brand awareness.

Our next engagement will start at the end of September, 2013. Please look for the announcement over the HGCC listserv, and keep an eye out for emails from us.

Eligibility & Requirements

  • Must be a graduate student (MBA, Masters, or PhD) or a post-doc.
  • No experience in business consulting needed.
  • No requirement on subject of study. We are looking for a team of 6 dedicated, creative thinkers across a broad range of disciplines

Description of Commitment

The engagement is intense and requires an 8 - 10 week commitment that involves large and small group meetings. Time commitment resembles that of part-time work and varies between 14 - 18 hours per week in addition to 2-hour weekly team meetings.

Attendance at the interviews and team meetings are mandatory. If you anticipate not being able to make an interview or plan to be away in the next few months, please let your interviewer know. We aim to accommodate the schedule of interested and committed individuals.
